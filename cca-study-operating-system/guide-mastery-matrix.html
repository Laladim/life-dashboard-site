CCA Guide Mastery Matrix

Source of truth: the official Claude Certified Architect - Foundations Certification Exam Guide, version 0.1, last updated Feb 10 2025.

Purpose: Track whether every guide-tested area is understandable, practiced, and exam-ready for Shela. Nothing is considered "passed" just because it was read or watched in a course.

Coverage count: 30 official guide task statements plus 6 official scenarios.

Status Rules

Use only these statuses:

  • Not started: not yet taught in trainer mode.
  • Explained: Shela heard/read a plain-English explanation.
  • Practiced: Shela attempted at least one active recall drill.
  • Passed: Shela answered correctly and explained why the wrong options are wrong.
  • Needs review: Shela missed, guessed, confused terms, or could not explain it back.

To mark a row Passed, add evidence in the Evidence column: date, drill type, score/result, and where it was logged.

Required Concept Card Format

Every guide topic must eventually have this card shape in notes, a lesson pack, or the trainer response:

  1. Plain meaning: one beginner sentence.
  2. Analogy: one real-world comparison.
  3. When you use it: one production scenario.
  4. Exam trap: the wrong answer shape.
  5. Correct instinct: the boring, well-bounded answer.
  6. Recall question: one question Shela answers without notes.
  7. Evidence: where the answer was graded/logged.
