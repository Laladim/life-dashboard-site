CCA Weekly Review Protocol

Purpose: Stop weak spots from disappearing into old notes. Every week should prove retention across domains, not just progress on new material.

Weekly Cadence

Run once every 5-7 study days, or before a mock exam.

Required Inputs

  • guide-mastery-matrix.md
  • progress.md
  • readiness-gates.md
  • mock-exam-bank.md
  • latest course reviewer results, if any

Review Steps

  1. Matrix stale-row scan
  • Find every Needs review row.
  • Find every Practiced row without evidence.
  • Pick the top 5 by exam weight and recent misses.
  1. Mixed recall
  • 10 vocabulary prompts.
  • 5 scenario classification prompts.
  • 5 spot-the-trap prompts.
  1. Mock MCQ mini-set
  • Pick 10 questions from mock-exam-bank.md.
  • Include at least one from each domain.
  • Shela must explain the correct answer and one wrong answer.
  1. Update mastery
  • Promote rows only when Shela explains without notes.
  • Demote rows to Needs review when she guesses, confuses domain labels, or cannot explain the trap.
  1. Next-week plan
  • Pick 3 rows to teach.
  • Pick 2 rows to re-drill.
  • Pick 1 scenario to apply.
