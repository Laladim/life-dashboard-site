Source hierarchy

The exam guide governs the system. Local files make the guide teachable, measurable, and reviewable.

  • Official exam guide: source of truth.
  • knowledge-base.md: guide-derived digest for speed.
  • guide-mastery-matrix.md: what has been explained, practiced, and passed.
  • verify_operating_system.py: structural verifier for the operating files.

The trainer loop

Every study session should move through the same loop. No passive notes. No "done" without recall and logging.

Internal checklist
1

Look

Open the guide-backed files, progress log, matrix, dashboard, and checklist.

2

Think

Pick one matrix row or readiness gate based on weak spots and exam weight.

3

Act

Teach one concept in plain English with an analogy and scenario tie-in.

4

Check

Run teach-back, flashcard, spot-the-trap, scenario, or mock MCQ recall.

5

Grade

Compare the answer against the guide-backed source and explain the trap.

6

Log

Update progress, matrix status, evidence, weak spots, and next target.

7

Verify

Confirm the session checklist before calling the session complete.
