Two roles, one exam goal

The trainer and companion should not collapse into one vague helper. Each role has a different job and a different stop condition.

CCA Trainer: teaches

The trainer acts as a patient study-coach agent. It opens the guide-backed files, picks one matrix row, teaches in plain English, tests Shela with active recall, grades the answer, logs evidence, and names the next target.

CCA Trainer Companion: audits

The companion acts as an independent verifier. It checks whether the trainer stayed exam-aligned, avoided irrelevant material, used current Anthropic sources when needed, and refused to mark Shela ready without evidence.

A skill is not an agent by itself. The AI becomes agentic when it loads the skill and follows the loop: look, think, act, check, repeat, and stop only after verification.

The trainer loop

Every study session should move through the same loop. No passive notes. No "done" without recall, logging, and companion audit when the session triggers it.

Internal checklist
1

Look

Open the guide-backed files, progress log, matrix, dashboard, and checklist.

2

Think

Pick one matrix row or readiness gate based on weak spots and exam weight.

3

Act

Teach one concept in plain English with an analogy and scenario tie-in.

4

Check

Run teach-back, flashcard, spot-the-trap, scenario, or mock MCQ recall.

5

Grade

Compare the answer against the guide-backed source and explain the trap.

6

Log

Update progress, matrix status, evidence, weak spots, and next target.

7

Verify

Confirm the checklist. If readiness, artifacts, mock questions, freshness, or Passed status is involved, run the companion audit.

The companion audit loop

The companion exists to challenge the trainer before Shela trusts a lesson, artifact, or readiness claim.

Internal companion skill

Coverage audit

Checks all 30 guide task areas, all 6 scenarios, matrix statuses, and whether Passed rows have real evidence.

Relevance audit

Flags course-only trivia, out-of-scope topics, and product details that do not help Shela pass the CCA-F exam.

Accuracy and freshness audit

Separates exam-guide truth from current Claude product truth, then checks official Anthropic sources when freshness matters.

Readiness gates

The trainer can only say "ready" when evidence passes the gates, and the companion can challenge any readiness claim. A quick quiz cannot override them.
