11. Post Templates (Content Playbook)

Template 1 — Personal Story Post

Opens with a vulnerable moment → paints the scene → names the inner critic → pivots with empathy → delivers the lesson through story → anchors to a scripture or faith truth → closes with a warm invitation. Best for: building trust and attracting your true audience.

Template 2 — "Wrong Mindset" Post

Names a common belief the audience holds → explains why it's keeping them stuck → reframes with a better mindset → ends with the action that comes from the new mindset. Best for: mindset pivots that drive shares.

Template 3 — Community Proof Post

Spotlights a member's before/after → shows the specific action that caused the transformation → credits the member by name → closes with the invitation for others to start their own journey. Best for: social proof without bragging.

Template 4 — Tactical Tips Post

Names the exact problem → lists the 3–5 steps to solve it → shows a quick example → gives the reader permission to start messy. Best for: guides, how-tos, save-worthy posts.

12. AI Prompt Library

Master Voice Prompt

ROLE: You are writing as Lala (Shela) — a Filipino freelancer, AI content systems builder, and Christ-centered teacher. VOICE: Vulnerable, empathetic, tactical, devotional. Warm but direct. Never guru-posture. STRUCTURE: Use the 7-step formula — Vulnerability → Scene → Inner Critic → Empathy Pivot → Lesson via Story → Faith Anchor → Warm Invitation. NEVER DO: - No hustle culture language - No income bragging or scarcity marketing - No "fake it till you make it" - No em-dashes - No guru tone ALWAYS DO: - Lead with heartwork, land with tactics - Name the inner critic by what she actually says - Anchor lessons to scripture or faith when natural - Use signature phrases where they fit - Close with a warm invitation, not a hard sell SIGNATURE PHRASES: "It's not about perfection, it's about direction" · "Follow someone ahead of you and help someone behind you" · "#BonafideFilipinoFreelancer" · "#smashthelovebutton" TASK: [insert the specific post brief here]

13. Interview & Call Scripts

"Can you guarantee my social media will grow?"

"First, we'll identify the main source of our traffic and effective strategies to convert our audience. We'll map out your digital marketing ecosystem. I cannot guarantee growth because it depends on industry competition and your unique value proposition, but I guarantee I will implement effective strategies, provide high-quality content, and do my best to convert traffic."

"What's your content creation process?"

"I start by developing a clear Message Framework — what we offer, why it matters. These become the foundation for topics. Before creating content, we define branding guidelines and visual identity so everything is consistent and reflects what makes your brand special."

"Tell me about a project showing your SMM skills"

"I developed my own social media page and implemented a comprehensive branding strategy — purpose, target audience, unique value proposition. By sharing engaging, valuable content, I grew my following and built a strong presence, demonstrating my ability to execute a strategic plan."

Cold Calling Flow

"I'm looking for the person at [Company] who manages [task]. We sent you an email with the details — did you receive it? Is there an email address we can send it to? Is it okay to get in touch with you in two days?"
